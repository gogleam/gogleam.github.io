What is our product?

Gleam helps you increase your mental health through phototherapy, which in result boost your mood and productivity without time commitment or lifestyle changes.

When to use it?

If you are not getting enough sunlight and struggling with one or more of the followings:

  • Feeling sad or down most of the day, nearly everyday
  • Feeling tired or having low energy
  • Losing interest in activities you once enjoyed
  • Withdrawing from social activities
  • Having difficulty concentrating
  • Experiencing carbohydrate cravings, overeating and weight gain
  • Oversleeping
  • Feeling hopeless, worthless, or guilty

Why you need it?

  • Anyone can enjoy the app no matter their gender, age, occupation, or location in the world. Gleam is built to be flexible and functional for anyone, without changing their lifestyle or needing any time commitment.
  • Keep track of everything and analyze your progress while using the app. You should see improvements in your mood and productivity.
  • Expand your circle by connecting with people from all across the globe. Share your stories and get the support you need to achieve your goals.
